# **App Name**: Lume Finance Academy

## Core Features:

- Course Marketplace: An interactive interface to browse NISM series certifications and advanced crypto trading courses with full syllabus visibility.
- AI Path Finder Tool: An AI-powered tool that evaluates a student's trading background and financial goals to recommend a personalized curriculum roadmap.
- Dynamic Consultation Scheduler: A booking UI for scheduling one-on-one sessions with trading mentors for personalized strategy reviews.
- Framer Motion Landing Experience: High-performance hero section with staggered entrance animations and hover effects inspired by professional trading terminals.
- Interactive Pricing Matrix: A responsive component displaying NISM series packages and ultimate crypto trading tiers with direct checkout integration.
- Live Stats & Social Proof: Automated tickers and counting components reflecting 1,000+ learners and successful trading placements.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Vibrant Zest Orange (#F26419) to evoke energy and financial growth, optimized for high visibility against a dark theme.
- Background color: Deep Obsidian Mocha (#14110F), providing a sophisticated, low-strain reading environment for long study sessions.
- Accent color: Sophisticated Cinnabar (#E5383B), used sparingly for high-value call-to-action buttons and alerts.
- Headline font: 'Playfair', a modern serif that conveys authority, vintage financial credibility, and high-end educational value.
- Body font: 'PT Sans', a humanist sans-serif chosen for its warmth and excellent legibility across dense educational content.
- Clean, thin-line Lucide-react icons (like Sparkles, ArrowRight, and TrendingUp) styled with gradients and subtle glassmorphism effects.
- Orchestrated Framer Motion transitions using the container-child stagger pattern for hero titles and list items to ensure a high-end feel.
